How to use FEMA Natural Risk Index for Natural Hazards (NRI) data: The FEMA NRI dataset is a planning tool for understanding a community's natural hazard risk. The percentiles above are based on historical data related to past incidents and baseline measures of expected annual loss, social vulnerability and community resilience at the U.S. county level. They are not predictive of future events but measure the amount of risk and resilience in your area were such an event to happen again.
